Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 19, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how CJ_Brain ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and discloses information in relation to the mobile application BLE Diagnostic Tool.

1. Information We Collect

  • Bluetooth Data: We access Bluetooth to scan and connect to BLE devices. No data is transmitted externally.
  • Local Storage: The app may store diagnosis logs or device information locally on your device. This data is not uploaded or shared.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data only for the purpose of enabling BLE communication and diagnostics within the app. We do not collect or store personal information, nor do we share any data with third parties.

3. Permissions

  • Bluetooth: Required to connect with nearby BLE devices.
  • Location (on some Android versions): Required to scan for BLE devices (as mandated by Android).
  • Storage: Used to store logs or temporary data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data from your device to external servers.
